**Under $t_\text{on}:$**

$$
v_{l, t_{on}} = V_{D}-V_{o}

$$
$$
\implies i_{L, t_{on}} = \frac{1}{L}\int_{0}^{t} V_{D}-V_{0} \, d\tau =(V_{D}-V_{o})t
$$
**Under $t_\text{off}:$**
$$
v_{l, t_{off}} = -V_{o}

$$
$$
\implies i_{L, t_{off}} = \frac{1}{L}\int_{t_{on}}^{t} -V_{0} \, d\tau =(-V_{o})(t-t_{on})
$$
